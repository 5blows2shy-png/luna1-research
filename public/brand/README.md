# Luna1 Research Brand Assets

These files are approved for public use when identifying or linking to Luna1 Research. Preserve clear space around the mark, keep proportions unchanged, and do not recolor the spectral lines.

- `luna1-prism.svg`: preferred scalable prism mark for light or neutral backgrounds.
- `luna1-prism-black.svg`: one-color mark for print, embossing, or restricted-color production.
- `luna1-prism-white.svg`: reversed one-color mark for dark backgrounds.
- `luna1-prism-transparent.png`: large transparent raster mark for software without SVG support.
- `luna1-logo-horizontal.svg` / `.png`: primary lockup for wide headers, documents, and partner pages.
- `luna1-logo-stacked.svg` / `.png`: compact lockup for square or portrait layouts.
- `luna1-social-card.png`: 1200×630 share image for social and link previews.
- `luna1-commercial-poster.png`: 1600×900 poster frame matching the Luna1 commercial identity.

The logo identifies independent educational investment research. Its use must not imply personalized financial advice, endorsement, or a brokerage relationship.
